About the Show

Policy doesn't change itself. Someone has to play the ground game.

Ground Game goes inside the rooms where psychedelic policy is actually being made — the state houses, the advocacy organizations, the clinics, and the courtrooms.

Each episode, Sam sits down with the lawmakers, researchers, advocates, and practitioners driving this movement forward. No hype. No shortcuts. Just the real work.
